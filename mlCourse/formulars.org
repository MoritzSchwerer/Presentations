* One variable
** Prediction

$$y_{pred} = 2 + 2.5x$$
$$y_{pred} = a + bx$$

#+REVEAL: split

| i | size   | y_pred | y     |
|---+--------+--------+-------|
| / | <>     | <>     | <>    |
| 1 | 100m^2 | 300    | 280   |

** Cost

$$J(\theta) = \frac{1}{m}\sum_{n=1}^{m}(y - y_{pred})^2$$

* Multiple variables
** Prediction

$$y_{pred} = \theta_0X_0 + \theta_1X_1 + \theta_2X_2 + \theta_nX_n$$
or
$$y_{pred} = \theta^T X$$
